Have an idea, spotted a bug, or need something fixed? Fill out the form below and your request will automatically become a tracked issue on our project board. Our AI will assess the risk level and route it to the right team. 🚀

Keep it short and descriptive (max 120 chars)

🤖 What happens next?

  1. 1 Your request becomes a GitHub Issue in the atl-meeting repo
  2. 2 It's added to the project board you selected
  3. 3 Our AI Risk Assessor evaluates severity (1–10) and assigns a risk label
  4. 4 The engineering team reviews and picks it up for the next sprint

🎯 Risk Scale

  • Critical 9–10 Security, data loss, outage
  • High 7–8 Workflow disruption, data integrity
  • Medium 4–6 Non-critical features, perf issues
  • Low 1–3 Cosmetic, docs, nice-to-haves
